Intimidation

The most critically applied theme. The model's design has to be seen as intimadating to a passerby and from a distance. The main tower's features are designed to have it represent an upsidedown missile that is impacting the ground, it is also made wide and of many straight lines and blocks that stick out of it's body, to look strong, stable and powerful. The secondary tower adds height and brings back the feeling of speed and agility with its narrow build.
